About Me

Angel Trinh was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. She graduated from University of California, Berkeley in May 2012, with a double major in Political Science and Media Studies. SHe spent her last semester of undergrad studying abroad in Rome, Italy and doing volunteer work in Maun, Botswana. She currently resides in New York City, and works in advertising.

In her free time Angel enjoys traveling, exploring the great outdoors, and learning how to code. Angel has been to a total of 31 countries and 6 continents within the past 4 years. She is currently learning how to code at Rutger's.
